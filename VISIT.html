HOW TO REACH UNIVERSITY

By Rail Those coming from other states of India by train, should get down at the Chennai Central Station, come out and cross the high road through the pedestrian subway and reach 'Park' suburban metro rail station. Two destination trains are available from Park suburban metro rail station - to (i) Tambaram (TBM) (ii) Chengalpattu (CGL). (i) If you take the train to Tambaram, then get down at Tambaram and reach the local bus stand there. Take T60 or T60 LSS bus and get down at VANDALUR stop. It is a 20 minute travel from Tambaram to reach BSAU University. (Total Travel time from Chennai Central Railway Station will be around One Hour). (ii) If you take the train to Chengalpattu, you can directly reach Vandalur railway station.BSAU University is just opposite to Vandalur railway station.

By Air The nearest railway station from the Chennai Airport is Thrisulam, located just opposite the Airport. Get on a train towards Tambaram and follow the instructions given in the 'By Rail' section above for reaching SRM University. Shuttle bus service is available from BSAU University main gate to commute around the university.
